Ancient Herbal Wisdom

The Power of Herbal Potlis

A Herbal Potli (bundle) is a traditional Ayurvedic technique where therapeutic herbs are bundled in muslin cloth, heated in medicated oils, and gently pressed onto the body. Experience nature's pharmacy at its finest.

🌿

What is Herbal Potli?

A Potli (meaning "small bundle" in Sanskrit) is made by tying a blend of medicated herbs, roots, and spices into a cloth pouch. This pouch is dipped in warm herbal oils and rhythmically stamped on the body, allowing the heat and herbs to penetrate deep into muscles and joints.
